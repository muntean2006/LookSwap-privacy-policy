1. Introduction

Welcome to LookTune, an application developed and operated by Rares Busuioc ("we," "our," or "us"). LookTune allows users to try out different hairstyles, beard styles, and makeup styles using AI-based image processing. It also provides an attractiveness rating and a **User Leaderboard** designed to help users identify which styles best suit their appearance.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how you can manage or delete your data.

By using LookTune, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

  • Your password is fully encrypted and managed by Firebase â€” we never have access to or visibility of your password at any time.

2.2. Photos and Camera Access

2.3. Uploaded Images

  • When you upload or capture a photo, it is temporarily stored on Google Cloud Storage and Firebase to process your hairstyle, beard, or makeup transformations.

  • Images that you **choose to upload to the public Leaderboard** are stored until you manually delete them or delete your account.

2.4. Facial Data Processing

Important Information About Facial Analysis:

When you use LookTune's transformation features (hairstyles, makeup, beard styles, or beauty score), your uploaded photo is analyzed by AI models to identify and process facial features. This facial analysis occurs automatically to enable accurate placement of virtual styles on your face.

What Facial Data is Processed:

  • The AI models analyze your photo to detect facial features and their positions (such as face shape, facial contours, and feature locations)
  • This analysis is performed solely to apply your requested transformation accurately
  • No biometric identification or facial recognition is performed â€” your face is not used to identify you across photos or build facial profiles

How Facial Data is Used:

  • Hairstyle Transformations: AI analyzes face position to place virtual hairstyles correctly
  • Makeup Application: AI detects facial features to apply virtual makeup appropriately
  • Beard Styling: AI identifies facial areas to apply virtual beard styles
  • Beauty Score: AI analyzes facial proportions to generate an attractiveness rating for entertainment purposes only (not a medical or health assessment)

Facial Data is NEVER Used For:

  • Identifying you or creating biometric profiles
  • Facial recognition across different photos
  • Marketing, advertising, or targeted ads
  • Training AI models or algorithm improvements
  • Any purpose beyond your explicit transformation request

Third-Party Processing:

Your photos are processed by secure third-party AI services to generate transformations. These services:

  • Process facial data only to fulfill your transformation request
  • Do not retain your photos or facial data after processing
  • Transmit all data using HTTPS encryption
  • Are not permitted to use your facial data for any other purpose

Facial Data Retention:

  • During Processing: Facial analysis occurs temporarily while your transformation is generated (typically 30-60 seconds)
  • After Processing: Your original photo and any facial analysis data are automatically deleted immediately after results are delivered to your device
  • Transformation Results: Only the final transformed images are stored temporarily (automatically deleted within 2 days)
  • User Control: You can delete transformation results immediately from the app's History screen
  • No Permanent Storage: LookTune does not permanently store facial analysis data or biometric information

Your Consent:

By using LookTune's transformation features, you consent to the temporary processing of facial data as described above. You may withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the app's transformation features or deleting your account.

Compliance with Biometric Privacy Laws:

LookTune complies with applicable biometric privacy regulations, including:

  • Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA)
  • Texas Capture or Use of Biometric Identifier Act (CUBI)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

3. Attractiveness Score and Leaderboard

LookTune provides an attractiveness score and allows users to opt-in to a public Leaderboard:

  • The **Attractiveness Score** is generated by a sophisticated AI model to quantify the attractiveness of your selected style.
  • Important: The Attractiveness Score is generated for entertainment and style guidance purposes only. It is not a medical assessment, health measurement, or indication of self-worth. Results are subjective AI-generated estimates.
  • The **Leaderboard** is a public feature where users can voluntarily upload their photos and scores to be viewed and ranked by other users.
  • We operate a content moderation system for the Leaderboard to detect and remove inappropriate content.
